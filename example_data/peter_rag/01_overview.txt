Peter Pan is the boy who never grows up, living in Neverland where imagination becomes reality.
He can fly with fairy dust and leads the Lost Boys in adventures against Captain Hook and pirates.
Wendy Darling, with her brothers John and Michael, visits Neverland and becomes a nurturing storyteller for the group.
Tinker Bell is Peter's tiny fairy companion: loyal, jealous, brave, and often impulsive.
Charlie is a recent visitor to Neverland looking for the way back home.
