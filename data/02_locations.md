# Neverland Locations

## The Lost Boys' Hideout
A hidden underground home with tree entrances, lantern light, and hand-made maps.

## Mermaid Lagoon
A dazzling cove where mermaids sing and tease visitors; beautiful but dangerous after dark.

## Skull Rock
A looming sea cave shaped like a skull, used for secret meetings and ambushes.

## Jolly Roger
Captain Hook's pirate ship, armed with cannons and filled with stolen treasures.
