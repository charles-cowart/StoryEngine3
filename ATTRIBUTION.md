This dataset includes material derived from public domain works by J. M. Barrie, including:

- "Peter and Wendy" (1911)
- "Peter Pan, or The Boy Who Wouldn't Grow Up" (1904)

These works are in the public domain in the United States.

This project does not use or include any material from later copyrighted adaptations
(such as the 1953 Disney film).

This dataset also includes material derived from the public domain work by Lewis Carroll:

- "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland" (1865)

This work is in the public domain in the United States.

This project does not use or include any material from later copyrighted adaptations
(such as the 1951 Disney film).
